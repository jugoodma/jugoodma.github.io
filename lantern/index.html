What is this?

Strava and Garmin (and probably other applications) provide services that let you stream your real-time location (as long as you take your phone with you) while you exercise. I'm re-creating this service open-source. Why? Well, I thought these services were premium/paid/subscription-based. Turns out, they're free. So I have no excuse to make this. We do it anyway!

If you're interested in self-hosting your own service like this, I'll have a full tutorial in the GitHub organization. It's not easy to set up, so good luck! (I'm kidding -- it's hard, but my documentation is good)
